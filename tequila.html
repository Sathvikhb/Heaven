What is Tequila?

Tequila is an alcoholic drink, and a distilled beverage that is manufactured using the fermented blue agave plant. Tequila is preferred by men and women of all drinking ages. It was first produced in the 16th century, and the oldest tequila is Grupo Jose Cuervo.

What is Tequila made from?
