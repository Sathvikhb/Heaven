All About Vodka

What is Vodka?

Vodka, originated from Europe, is a distilled alcoholic beverage that is present in colorless form. There are different types of vodka, and the best vodka drinks to pair with your meal or have it after a party. On GotoLiquorStore, you can explore vodka, top shelf vodka, potato vodka brands, Russian vodka brands, and more from the vodka brands list that is available before you place your order.

Where does Vodka come from?

Vodka has been present in the liquor industry since the 14th century. The vodka belongs to Russia, and Poland, and after World War II, it became popular in the United States of America as well.

How is Vodka made?

Vodka is made using different ingredients or grains like corn, rice, wheat, rye, molasses, and fruits. To make vodka, malt meal, water, and yeast are added together, and the grained scratch gets converted into sugar.

What does Vodka taste like?

As vodka is made using different types of grains, on the sip, you get different tastes of grains, and you can easily identify the ingredients present in it. You can easily search for the most popular vodka brands from the nearby liquor stores.
