All About Wine

What is Wine?

Wine is an alcoholic beverage fermented with grape juice. The contents of wine are usually water, alcohol, acid, sugar, and phenolic compounds like anthocyanins, tannins, and vanilla. Wine color and general style are known by the type of grape used and the amount of skin contact during juice extraction. There is no relation between wine color and its sweetness. The flavor of the wine will also change by the region where grapes are grown.

Popular Types of Wine

The two most popular types include white wine and red wine. These types further divide into sub-types, giving options to drink different tastes. Red wine types include cabernet sauvignon, pinot noir, merlot, syrah/shiraz, and more. Cabernet Sauvignon with dark fruit flavors is a rich red wine. White wine types include chardonnay, sauvignon blanc, moscato, riesling, and more. Chardonnay is a favorite of wine specialists for its citrusy notes and tropical flavors. Sparkling wine is for celebratory moments to enjoy the special occasion. Types of sparkling wines are white, rose, brut, and more. If you want to drink something sweet, then the Rose & Blush wine must be on your list! Given the palate of vanilla, rose, and flavors of fruits, the rose & blush wine is perfect for a gift or to have on a romantic occasion. You should also try one of the many flavored dessert wines.

Food Pairings with Wine

Wine and great food create a balance between a dish's components and a wine's characteristics. General tips for pairing your favorite wine with the food items are as follows; Wine should be more acidic than your food Wine should be sweeter than your food Flavors of wine and food should match up to an extent Red wine pairs best with red meat White wine pairs best fish or chicken Dessert or cake goes with sweet wine
